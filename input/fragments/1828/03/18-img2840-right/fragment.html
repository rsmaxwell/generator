A fine mild day. Wind SSW. The surveyors of the British & Hope clubs on board They thought we ought to get a new mainstay sail & that we ought also to have a new towline. Bound two boys E Read for 6 years at £45 & G Floss for 3 years at 30. Dined on board & went to a party at W C Wrights in the evening. Paid Gibson butchers £8.7/ & Hall £3.13/- & self cash £5
